We propose a multi-task convolutional neural network operating on Harmonic Constant-Q Transform (HCQT) representations, with Hidden Markov Model post-processing for temporal smoothing, for the task of violin melody extraction from polyphonic music signals.

[Download the paper (PDF)](/files/multitask_CNNs_melodyextraction.pdf)

**Authors:** David W. Fong, Patrick A. Naylor (Imperial College London).
