**Role:** Founder (Audio GenAI)
**Period:** April 2024 – December 2025
**Organisation:** SoundBerry (backed by CDP Venture Capital)

Shipped a B2C generative music product focused on stem-level creation, iterating on model and UX based on user feedback.

- Designed diffusion-based audio-to-audio models using CLAP-based conditioning and latent representations.
- Trained 5B+ parameter models on a multi-TB dataset; evaluated with contrastive metrics and prompt testing.
- Deployed an API-based inference pipeline for model serving and batch audio generation to the platform.
- Led design partner integrations to define pilots, secure IP-compliant datasets, and validate product-market fit.

**Stack:** PyTorch, Diffusers, CLAP, FastAPI, Docker, AWS/GCP.
