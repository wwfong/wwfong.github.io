DAACI is a meta-composition company spun out of the Centre for Digital Music at Queen Mary University of London. As Product Manager I owned the bridge between research and product, scoping requirements, and translating research prototypes into solutions targeted at gaming, library music, and film/TV use cases.

The flagship product I shepherded end-to-end was an AI generative drum copilot for DAW-based workflows: I orchestrated the full development lifecycle, owned creation of system documentation derived from the research prototypes, and co-authored key patent filings around the underlying meta-composition techniques.

**Role:** Product Manager (Meta-Composition).
**Themes:** generative drums, DAW integration, meta-composition, research-to-product, IP and patents.
