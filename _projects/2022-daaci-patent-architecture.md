I contributed to the system architecture of DAACI's meta-composition engine: the layer that turns abstract musical structures into renderable musical material across instruments and contexts. The architecture had to support deterministic reproducibility for studio workflows, real-time generation for game and copilot use cases, and clean interfaces between the symbolic composition layer and the rendering layer.

In parallel I co-authored DAACI's foundational [patent filings](https://patents.google.com/patent/WO2022207765A3/) covering the underlying meta-composition methods. The filing work involved translating research prototypes into precise claims, distinguishing the approach from prior art, and working with patent counsel through the prosecution process.

**Role:** Product Manager (Meta-Composition); patent co-author.

**Themes:** system architecture, generative music, meta-composition, intellectual property, patents.
