In the early months of the pandemic, with conferences and in-person mentoring routes shut down, we launched RED Turntables: an open mentorship programme connecting music tech founders with the network at Abbey Road REDD and Universal Music. I helped set up the programme, recruit mentors from across the group and the wider industry, and run the sessions.

Read the [week-one recap](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-launches-red-turntables-week-one-recap-2719).

**Role:** Co-organiser.

**Themes:** mentorship, founder support, COVID-era community building.
