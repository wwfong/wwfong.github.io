I supported Universal Music's acquisition of [Audiomovers](https://audiomovers.com/), the real-time audio collaboration platform whose Listento and Omnibus tools are widely used by mixing and mastering engineers. My role spanned commercial and technical due diligence, positioning of Audiomovers within the broader Abbey Road and Universal Music audio strategy, and post-deal integration planning.
