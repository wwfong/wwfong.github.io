XONE is a metaverse platform for music experiences. We accepted them into Abbey Road REDD's incubation programme and I worked with the founders on product direction, partnership development inside Universal Music, and the commercial and creative milestones that mattered for their next stage.

Read the [Abbey Road incubation announcement](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-announces-metaverse-platform-xone-as-its-newest-start-up-3216).
