A meaningful slice of my work at Abbey Road REDD was knowledge sharing aimed at the broader music tech industry. REDD was Europe's first dedicated music tech incubator and the innovation arm of Universal Music Group. Output split into three streams.

**Editorial pieces.**

- [Music at Long Distance: Online Creation and Collaboration Tools](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/music-at-long-distance-online-creation-and-collaboration-tools-from-abbey-road-red-2704), an analysis of the tools artists and producers were turning to during the early lockdown period.
- [Music at Long Distance: Artificial Intelligence Tools](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/music-at-long-distance-artificial-intelligence-tools-2709), the AI follow-up to the same shift.
- [A Historical Overview of Background Music](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/a-historical-overview-of-background-music-from-abbey-road-red-2833), a longer-form piece tracing background music from Muzak through to streaming-era functional audio.
- [The Positive Neuro-Physiological Effects of Music](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/the-positive-neuro-physiological-effects-of-music-from-abbey-road-reds-karim-fanous-2743), a deep-dive on the science by my colleague Karim Fanous, published in the same series.

**RED Talks.** The talk series REDD ran with researchers, founders, and artists, with public recaps.

- [A Primer on Brain-Computer Interfaces for Music](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-talk-recap-a-primer-on-brain-computer-interfaces-for-music-3196).
- Satellite of Creation, a two-part series on how online music-making is evolving: [part one](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-talk-satellite-of-creation-how-our-music-making-is-evolving-online-3050) and [part two](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-talk-satellite-of-creation-how-our-music-making-is-evolving-online-blog-two-3056).
- [To the Metaverse and Beyond: What's Next for Gen Z and Music](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-talk-to-the-metaverse-and-beyond-whats-next-for-gen-z-and-music-2934).

**Annual forecasts.** The 10 Trends and 10 to Watch series, REDD's yearly read on where music tech was heading.

- [10 Trends for 2021: Voice Activation and Social Audio](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-10-trends-for-2021-red-talk-voice-activation-and-social-audio-3011).
- [10 Trends for 2021: Immersive Audio and High-Tech Hearables](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-red-10-trends-for-2021-red-talk-immersive-audio-and-high-tech-hearables-3007).
- [10 to Watch in 2021: The Countdown (10 to 3)](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/abbey-road-reds-10-to-watch-in-2021-the-countdown-10-3-2940), the annual list of music tech companies worth following.

**Role:** Author, editor, and contributor.

**Themes:** audio technology, AI in music, online music collaboration, metaverse, immersive audio, brain-computer interfaces, music tech foresight.
