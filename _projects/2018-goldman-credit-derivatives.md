I joined Goldman's Credit Derivatives desk as a Trading Analyst. The day-to-day was estimating default probabilities and using them to price and execute trades across more than 150 reference names, with daily traded volume north of $100 million. The book spanned single-name CDS and index instruments, with the supporting analytics that fed pricing decisions under fast market conditions.

**Role:** Trading Analyst (Credit Derivatives).

**Themes:** credit risk, default probability modelling, derivatives pricing, systematic execution.
