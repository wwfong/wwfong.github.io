I joined Goldman Sachs' Credit Derivatives desk as a Trading Analyst. My day-to-day work was estimating default probabilities and using them to price and execute trades across more than 150 reference names, with daily traded volume north of $100 million. The role spanned single-name CDS, index instruments, and the supporting analytics that fed into pricing decisions under fast market conditions.

**Role:** Trading Analyst (Credit Derivatives).

**Themes:** credit risk, default probability modelling, derivatives pricing, systematic execution.
