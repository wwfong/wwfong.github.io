Each month I helped prepare a research briefing for Universal Music Group leadership, covering notable signals in audio technology, music AI, music tech investment, and adjacent shifts in consumer behaviour. The briefings were both an internal advisory product giving executives context for strategic decisions, and a public artefact: a public version was published on the REDD blog.

UMG is the world's largest music label by market share, so the briefings were a quiet but useful way to keep leadership current on the parts of the music tech landscape that mattered for the group's direction.
