**Role:** Researcher (BEng Thesis), supervised by Prof. Patrick A. Naylor
**Period:** 2018
**Organisation:** Imperial College London — Department of Electrical &amp; Electronic Engineering

Designed and evaluated a multi-task convolutional neural network operating on Harmonic Constant-Q Transform (HCQT) representations, with Hidden Markov Model post-processing for temporal smoothing, applied to violin melody extraction from polyphonic music signals. Awarded First Class with prize; the work was submitted to ISMIR 2019 (see [publication](/publication/2019-multitask-cnns-violin-melody)).

**Stack:** Python, Keras, Librosa, HCQT, HMMs.
