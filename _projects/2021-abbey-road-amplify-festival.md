For Abbey Road Studios' 90th anniversary I helped organise [Abbey Road Amplify](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/announcing-abbey-road-amplify-our-90th-anniversary-festival-3057), the festival celebrating the studio's history and future. REDD's contribution was [the Playroom](https://www.abbeyroad.com/news/announcing-the-abbey-road-red-playroom-as-part-of-our-amplify-festival-3064), an experiential showcase of music technology where visitors could try out emerging audio tools, instruments, and immersive experiences hands-on.

I scoped the Playroom programme, coordinated with the partner companies whose tools were on display, and worked with the broader event team on production logistics.

**Role:** Programming lead for the Playroom.

**Themes:** experiential events, music tech showcase, audience engagement, partnerships.
